A room based on Cornell Box. The scene includes realistic lighting and shadows, with walls acting as secondary light sources. It can also be viewed in VR. A GUI is integrated to adjust material properties, lighting conditions, and shadow settings, enhancing the interactivity of the VR environment.

Color Space in VR

This project involves creating a real-time 3D, VR, and MR application for visualizing color data from a video file. The application displays a dynamic color cloud in RGB, CIExyY, and CIELAB color spaces, with options for density visualization. Users can interact with the system using a Meta Quest headset to manipulate the video and color cloud. Additionally, a movable video playback plane has been implemented, allowing users to reposition it within the virtual space for enhanced interaction.

About Me

Hello! I am a student studying imaging and light in extended reality. I am specializing in 3D rendering and image processing.

My work focuses on pushing the boundaries of what's possible with WebGL and Three.js, creating performant and visually stunning experiences for the web.

Beyond 3D graphics, I have a deep interest in video editing, blending storytelling with visual effects to create immersive narratives. When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring new technologies, finding new open-source projects, or working out at the gym :).
